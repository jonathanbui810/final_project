KNOWING HOW MUCH GOES WHERE


Using Real Data to Show Where Spending is Going in Prince George’s County


Our web application allows you to get information about government spending in Prince George’s County Maryland and compare that data to the spending in other departments and from fiscal year 17 (FY17) and fiscal year 18 (FY18). This helps you see which departments have the most or least spending and analyze the changes between years or the differences between departments.
